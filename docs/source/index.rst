How to use Python to multiply your **Crypto** assets
====================================================

:doc:`Python`-- a flexible and rich language as that-- can 
help you dig out your assets in cryptocoins, and use them
for buying (when the price is low) and selling (as prices
creep up).

For that you need :doc:`ta`-- studying the market, and 
deciding the trends, averages, and top times to buy/sell
your assets.
